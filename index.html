The Story

Follow Roli's Journey

To herald the women's month this March, Laju Iren Films presents Onobiren: A Woman's Story.

Follow Roli's journey from Warri to Lagos — when what begins as a familiar narrative quickly transforms when she chooses a different path, one driven by purpose, survival, and self-discovery; powered by fishing roots and the magic that happens when women support women.

What People Are Saying

Audience Reactions

Mrs Tara Fela-Durotoye

Fabulous, fabulous! A wonderful film that promotes intuitive enterprise amongst women, celebrates the men who support them, while beautifully portraying the Itsekiri culture and the riverine region. It brings back memories from my own childhood!

Dr Morenike Molehin

Onobiren is such a beautiful film. The storyline is fantastic with incredible plot twists. The cinematography and overall quality of the production are out of this world. If a film could feel like a warm hug, this would be it!

Kemi Onabanjo-Joseph

It is a beautiful film, and a truly unconventional story that would take you by surprise. I love that there are many lessons that can be gleaned from it — even if your situation is not exactly like that of the main character.

Olushola Olaleye

Absolutely gripping. It carried you through every emotion: sadness, humor, ecstasy, and the sweet taste of victory. A definite must-watch! A gentle reminder that what you're looking for could already be within you.
